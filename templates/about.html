About Me

This is what I do.

Hey there! I'm Ilan, a 20-year-old software engineering student from Nicaragua. I'm currently in my third year at UCA, where I'm pursuing my passion for technology and programming. My primary focus is on front-end and back-end development, with Python being my language of choice.

Throughout my academic journey, I've gained a solid foundation in software engineering principles and practices. I enjoy the process of crafting intuitive user interfaces and implementing robust back-end functionality. When I'm not immersed in coding or studying, I make sure to stay updated on the latest advancements in the field. I actively seek out opportunities to apply my skills in real-world scenarios, whether through personal projects or collaborations with fellow students. I'm excited to connect with like-minded individuals and contribute to innovative projects that push the boundaries of technology.
